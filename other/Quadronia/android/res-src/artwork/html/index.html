How to Play

Find the Rectangles

Spot rectangular areas on the board where all four corners share the same color. The bigger the rectangle, the higher your score.

Click to Clear

Tap two diagonal corners of the rectangle to clear all the tiles inside. Quick thinking and pattern recognition are key to survival.

Collect Bonuses

Clocks - more time to think Stars - match any color Multipliers - double your score Coins - unlock powerful abilities

Use Power-Ups

Bombs - blast parts of the field Reshuffles - change the field completely Hints - reveal the best move
